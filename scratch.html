Why I Love Skiing

There's nothing quite like the feeling of dropping into a fresh powder run on a bluebird morning — skis floating through untouched snow, cold air rushing past, and the entire mountain to yourself. Skiing isn't just a hobby for me; it's the thing I look forward to all year.

Growing up I learned to ski on smaller hills, but once I got a taste of real terrain in Utah and Colorado, I was completely hooked. Park City, Snowbird, and Jackson Hole are my home mountains — there's something about speed, technique, and nature that keeps pulling me back.

Favorite memory: Dropping into the steeps at Snowbird on a January powder day with 18" of fresh snow — nothing beats it.
